Cape Clear is a microcosm

of the housing crisis at large


This island community is dying because there are no homes to rent or to buy. This is despite the island being full of empty houses.

The island has become a holiday resort and retirement home due to this lack of housing for newcomers. The school is about to close and the population is in a free-fall.

Despite this fact, some people are choosing to hoard homes that could be made available to the many families who would like to have them.

It is our aim to document each and every empty home on the island of Cape Clear and, with you help, the entire island of Ireland in the hope that by identifying the problem of vacant housing in Ireland, we can encourage change.
