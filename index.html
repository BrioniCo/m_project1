Cape Clear Island


Dozens of vacant homes in a dying island community


Ireland's housing crisis contained in 3 square miles


Click here to view the house you'll never have

Cape Clear island

is a microcosm of the housing crisis in Ireland

This small island community is dying because there are no homes to rent or to buy

There are plenty of empty houses on this island


Some are just choosing to hoard houses that should be homes


Our aim is to document each and every vacant home on the island of Cape Clear

and the entire island of Ireland

Our hope is that this will encourage the owners to put them back into use
